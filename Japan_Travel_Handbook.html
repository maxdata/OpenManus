Japan Travel Handbook

7-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1-3: Explore Tokyo (Tsukiji and Toyosu Fish Markets, shrines, temples, cocktail bars).
  • Day 4-5: Kyoto (shrines, palaces, cultural heritage).
  • Day 6: Nara (deer park, historical sites).
  • Day 7: Return to Tokyo for final exploration.

Proposal Locations

  • Kyoto: Serene temples and gardens.
  • Nara: Picturesque parks with deer.

Essential Japanese Phrases

  • Hello: Konnichiwa (こんにちは)
  • Thank you: Arigatou (ありがとう)
  • Excuse me: Sumimasen (すみません)
  • Where is...: ...wa doko desu ka? (...はどこですか？)

Travel Tips

  • Purchase a Japan Rail Pass for convenient travel.
  • Carry cash, as some places may not accept cards.
  • Respect local customs and etiquette.
